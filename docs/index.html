Features

Everything you need to stay on track

Designed to be simple and powerful — no bloat, just the tools that actually help.

📋

Smart Task List

Add tasks with priorities, categories, time slots, and notes. Tasks auto-sort by priority and time so the most important things are always at the top.

🗓️

Schedule View

Visualize your day as hourly time blocks from 6 AM to 10 PM. See a live indicator of the current time and plan your day like a pro.

📊

Progress Tracking

A live progress bar shows how much of your day you've completed. Watch it fill up as you check off tasks — satisfying every time.

🔄

Cross-Device Sync

Create an account and your tasks sync instantly between web and mobile. Start planning on your computer, check off tasks on your phone.

🌙

Dark Mode

Easy on the eyes day or night. Toggle dark mode from any screen — your preference is saved automatically.

📅

Day Navigation

Browse any day — past, present, or future. Plan tomorrow's tasks today, or review what you accomplished last week.
